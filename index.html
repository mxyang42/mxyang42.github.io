About me

I'm currently a postdoc at Kavli IPMU. Prior to this I was a postdoc at Heidelberg University mentored by Anna Wienhard. I graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago under the supervision of Laura Schaposnik. I received my Bachelor degree from University of Waterloo, with a double major in Pure Math and Computer Science.

Recently I'm interested in $G^\mathbb{R}$-Higgs bundles, higher Teichmüller components and branes. Broadly speaking I am interested in understanding the geometry of Higgs bundles and flat connections on Riemann surfaces.

I co-organized the Workshop and school on Complex Lagrangians, Integrable Systems, and Quantization (Oxford, Jun 2023) with Christopher Mahadeo and Laura Schaposnik. There are plans for another workshop about Higgs bundles in fall 2026, stay tuned.

"Meng-Xue" roughly sounds like "MUNG-SHOO-EY". It means "dream-snow" in Mandarin.
